Information

  • Please do not bring hot food into the auditoriums.
  • Present your over 65 ID and you can purchase one discount ticket each time. The discount ticket will require proof of identification when collecting tickets and is not available for Royal Club.
  • Present your Disabled ID card and you can purchase two disabled discount tickets each time. The disabled discount ticket will require proof of identification when collecting tickets and is not available for Royal Club.
  • Please present a valid concession ID when purchasing concession tickets.
  • Children above the height of 90cm must purchase tickets.
  • Please check your tickets and count your change before leaving the counter. The cinema will not be responsible thereafter.
  • Refunds and exchange of tickets will only be accepted 20 minutes prior to the advertised session time.
